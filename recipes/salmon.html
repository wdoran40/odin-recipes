Salmon Cakes

Description

Let me tell ya, Chef John knew what he was doing when he made these cakes. They are so fresh and juicy, I guarantee there won't be any leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Olive Olive
  • Onion
  • Salmon
  • Mayonaise
  • Capers
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Bell Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat the oil over medium heat. Stir in onion, pepper, celery, and salt. Add capers for about 2 min. Remove from heat.
  2. Stir slamon, onion mixture into mayo, bread crumbs, garlic, and every other seasoning. Place in fridge for an hour.
  3. Form patties.
  4. Put patties in oil over medium heat. For a few minutes each side.
