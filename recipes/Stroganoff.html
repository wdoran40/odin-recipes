Beef Stroganoff

Description

This Beef Stroganoff might as well come from another planet. Your tastebuds will soar while your body stays grounded eating this beautiful comfort food.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • oil
  • onion
  • ground beef
  • garlic
  • flour
  • broth
  • noodles

Directions

  1. Oil a skillet and cook onions and beef
  2. Stir in garlic and flour.
  3. Stir in noodles.
