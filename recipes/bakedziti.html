Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti recipe will make you shiver in sheer delight. With it's profound and robust flavor profile, the crowd will be begging for more.

Ingredients

  • italian sausage
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • oregano
  • thyme
  • rosemary
  • marinara
  • ziti

Directions

  1. cut sausage
  2. heat oil and cook sausage with seasonings
  3. add sauce and let simmer
  4. cook pasta and add to dish. Add the meat sauce.
  5. spread cheese and bake for 20 minutes.
